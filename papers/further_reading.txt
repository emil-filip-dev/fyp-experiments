Organised by theme. URLs are arXiv/DOI links — verify before citing.

1. SAFE RL & SHADOW MODE (Fallback / Intervention Mechanisms)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Garcia, J. & Fernandez, F. (2015)
  "A Comprehensive Survey on Safe Reinforcement Learning"
  Journal of Machine Learning Research, 16, 1437–1480
  https://jmlr.org/papers/v16/garcia15a.html
  [Foundational survey — taxonomises safe RL into teacher advice, shielding,
   risk-sensitive objectives, constrained MDPs. Essential background.]

Dalal, G., Dvijotham, K., Vecerik, M., Hester, T., Paduraru, C. & Tassa, Y. (2018)
  "Safe Exploration in Continuous Action Spaces"
  arXiv:1801.08757
  https://arxiv.org/abs/1801.08757
  [Adds a safety layer on top of any policy: projects actions onto safe set
   via linear constraints. Closest prior work to shadow mode's intervention idea.]

Wabersich, K.P. & Zeilinger, M.N. (2021)
  "A Predictive Safety Filter for Learning-Based Control of Constrained Nonlinear
   Dynamical Systems"
  Automatica, 129, 109639
  https://doi.org/10.1016/j.automatica.2021.109639
  [MPC-based safety filter that overrides unsafe RL actions — conceptually
   similar to Q-value switching but uses reachability analysis instead.]

Brunke, L., Greeff, M., Hall, A.W., Yuan, Z., Zhou, S., Panerati, J. & Schoellig, A.P. (2022)
  "Safe Learning in Robotics: From Learning-Based Control to Safe Reinforcement Learning"
  Annual Review of Control, Robotics, and Autonomous Systems, 5, 411–444
  https://doi.org/10.1146/annurev-control-042920-020211
  [Excellent survey connecting CBF, MPC safety filters, and safe RL.
   Useful for framing shadow mode within the robotics/control safety literature.]

Pecka, M. & Svoboda, T. (2014)
  "Safe Exploration Techniques for Reinforcement Learning — An Overview"
  IROS Workshop on Modelling, Estimation, Perception and Control of All Terrain Mobile Robots
  https://arxiv.org/abs/1412.2101
  [Early survey of safe exploration methods; good historical context.]

Thananjeyan, B., Balakrishna, A., Niekum, S., Luo, M., Srinivas, A. & Goldberg, K. (2021)
  "Recovery RL: Safe Reinforcement Learning with Learned Recovery Zones"
  IEEE RA-L 2021, arXiv:2010.15920
  https://arxiv.org/abs/2010.15920
  [Trains a recovery policy that takes over near constraint boundaries —
   complementary to shadow mode: instead of reverting to baseline, it learns
   a dedicated escape policy. Good ablation comparator.]


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
2. CONSTRAINED RL (CMDPs, Safety Constraints)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Tessler, C., Mankowitz, D.J. & Mannor, S. (2018)
  "Reward Constrained Policy Optimization"
  ICLR 2019, arXiv:1805.11074
  https://arxiv.org/abs/1805.11074
  [RCPO: multi-timescale Lagrangian with penalty signal propagated through
   the return. More stable than vanilla Lagrangian — strong baseline for
   constraint-aware shadow mode (Extension 1).]

Achiam, J., Held, D., Tamar, A. & Abbeel, P. (2017)
  "Constrained Policy Optimization"
  ICML 2017, arXiv:1705.10528
  https://arxiv.org/abs/1705.10528
  [CPO: first policy gradient algorithm with rigorous constraint satisfaction
   guarantees. Solves constrained CMDP directly. Highly cited baseline.]

Stooke, A., Achiam, J. & Abbeel, P. (2020)
  "Responsive Safety in Reinforcement Learning by PID Lagrangian Methods"
  ICML 2020, arXiv:2007.03964
  https://arxiv.org/abs/2007.03964
  [PID-Lagrangian: adaptive Lagrange multiplier via PID control law.
   Dramatically stabilises Lagrangian RL — directly relevant to constraint
   reward shaping in Extension 1.]

Petsagkourakis, P., Sandoval, I.O., Bradford, E., Zhang, D. & del Rio-Chanona, E.A. (2020)
  "Reinforcement Learning for Batch Bioprocess Optimization"
  Computers & Chemical Engineering, 133, 106649
  https://doi.org/10.1016/j.compchemeng.2019.106649
  [Constrained RL for batch bioprocess — PSE application close to CSTR setting.
   Uses Lagrangian penalty + DDPG.]

Petsagkourakis, P., Sandoval, I.O., Bradford, E., Galvanin, F., Zhang, D. &
del Rio-Chanona, E.A. (2022)
  "Safe Policy Optimization with Local Gaussian Process Regression"
  Computers & Chemical Engineering, 165, 107932
  https://doi.org/10.1016/j.compchemeng.2022.107932
  [Combines GP uncertainty with RL for safe exploration in PSE. Good companion
   to Extension 3 (uncertainty-quantified switching).]

Ray, A., Achiam, J. & Amodei, D. (2019)
  "Benchmarking Safe Exploration in Deep Reinforcement Learning"
  (Safety Gym paper)
  https://arxiv.org/abs/1910.01708
  [Introduces Safety Gym benchmark. Useful to compare against PC-Gym for
   constraint satisfaction evaluation methodology.]

3. LEARNING TO DEFER (Human-AI / Expert-AI Teaming)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Mozannar, H. & Sontag, D. (2020)
  "Consistent Estimators for Learning to Defer to an Expert"
  ICML 2020, arXiv:2006.01862
  https://arxiv.org/abs/2006.01862
  [Foundational L2D paper. Consistent surrogate loss for joint training of
   classifier + deferral rule. One-shot (non-sequential) but key theoretical
   foundation for SLTD.]

Verma, R., Nalisnick, E. & Ghahramani, Z. (2022)
  "Calibrated Learning to Defer with One-vs-All Classifiers"
  ICML 2022
  https://arxiv.org/abs/2206.10668
  [Addresses calibration of deferral confidence. Directly relevant to
   threshold η_agent in shadow mode's agent-decision switching mode.]

Madras, D., Pitassi, T. & Zemel, R. (2018)
  "Predict Responsibly: Improving Fairness and Accuracy of Imperfect Human
   Annotations"
  NeurIPS 2018
  https://arxiv.org/abs/1711.04219
  [Selectively defers to human annotator when ML model uncertain.
   Predecessor framework to L2D.]

Charusaie, M.A., Mozannar, H., Sontag, D. & Samadi, S. (2022)
  "Sample Efficient Learning of Predictors that Complement Humans"
  ICML 2022
  https://arxiv.org/abs/2207.10153
  [Extends L2D to the sample-efficient regime — relevant to PSE where data
   collection on real plant is expensive.]

Hemmer, P., Thede, L., Vössing, M. & Kühl, N. (2023)
  "Toward Human-AI Collaboration in Sequential Decision Making"
  arXiv:2307.04390
  https://arxiv.org/abs/2307.04390
  [Extends L2D to sequential settings with non-stationarity — direct bridge
   between SLTD and shadow mode's deferral framing. Very recent and relevant.]

4. HYBRID RL + CLASSICAL CONTROL
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Silver, T., Allen, K., Tenenbaum, J. & Kaelbling, L. (2018)
  "Residual Policy Learning"
  arXiv:1812.06298
  https://arxiv.org/abs/1812.06298
  [Train RL to output residual correction to a baseline policy (e.g. PID).
   Alternative to shadow mode — complementary baseline for evaluation.]

Johannink, T., Bahl, S., Nair, A., Luo, J., Kumar, A., Lober, M. & Levine, S. (2019)
  "Residual Reinforcement Learning for Robot Control"
  ICRA 2019, arXiv:1812.03201
  https://arxiv.org/abs/1812.03201
  [Residual RL applied to robotic manipulation. Demonstrates that RL on top
   of a weak prior policy converges faster and more stably.]

Lawrence, N.P., Forbes, M.G., Loewen, P.D., MacDonald, D.G., Backström, J.U. &
Gopaluni, R.B. (2022)
  "Deep Reinforcement Learning with Shallow Controllers: An Experimental Application
   to PID Tuning"
  Control Engineering Practice, 121, 105046
  https://doi.org/10.1016/j.conengprac.2021.105046
  [RL for online PID gain scheduling — alternative hybrid approach to CIRL.
   Useful comparison point for Extension 2.]

Dogru, O., Velswamy, K., Ibrahim, F., Wu, Y., Sundaramoorthy, A.S., Huang, B.,
Xu, S., Nixon, M. & Bell, N. (2022)
  "Reinforcement Learning Approach to Autonomous PID Tuning"
  Computers & Chemical Engineering, 161, 107801
  https://doi.org/10.1016/j.compchemeng.2022.107801
  [RL-tuned PID on real industrial process (Emerson DeltaV). Demonstrates
   practical deployment — useful motivation for why hybrid RL+PID matters.]

Degrave, J., Felici, F., Buchli, J., et al. (2022)
  "Magnetic Control of Tokamak Plasmas through Deep Reinforcement Learning"
  Nature, 602, 414–419
  https://doi.org/10.1038/s41586-021-04301-9
  [Landmark real-world RL application in control of a physical system with
   stringent safety constraints. Useful motivation for industrial deployment.]

5. CHEMICAL PROCESS CONTROL WITH RL
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Spielberg, S.P.K., Gopaluni, R.B. & Loewen, P.D. (2017)
  "Deep Reinforcement Learning Approaches for Process Control"
  ADCHEM 2017
  https://doi.org/10.1016/j.ifacol.2017.08.1514
  [One of the first demonstrations of deep RL (DQN, DDPG) for PID setpoint
   control in process industries. Foundational PSE-RL paper.]

Nian, R., Liu, J. & Huang, B. (2020)
  "A Review on Reinforcement Learning: Introduction and Applications in
   Industrial Process Control"
  Computers & Chemical Engineering, 139, 106886
  https://doi.org/10.1016/j.compchemeng.2020.106886
  [Comprehensive review of RL in industrial process control up to 2020.
   Good background for framing the FYP contribution.]

Mowbray, M., Petsagkourakis, P., del Rio-Chanona, E.A. & Zhang, D. (2022)
  "Safe Chance Constrained Reinforcement Learning for Batch Process Control"
  Computers & Chemical Engineering, 157, 107630
  https://doi.org/10.1016/j.compchemeng.2021.107630
  [Chance-constraint approach to safe RL in batch processes — formally handles
   probabilistic state constraints. Very relevant to Extension 1.]

Bradford, E., Imsland, L., Zhang, D. & del Rio-Chanona, E.A. (2020)
  "Stochastic Data-Driven Model Predictive Control Using Gaussian Processes"
  Computers & Chemical Engineering, 139, 106844
  https://doi.org/10.1016/j.compchemeng.2020.106844
  [GP-MPC for process control — baseline comparator to shadow mode + MPC
   safety filter combination in Extension 1.]

Yang, S., Wan, M.P., Chen, W., Ng, B.F. & Dubey, S. (2021)
  "Model-Based Safe Reinforcement Learning with Lyapunov Functions for
   HVAC Control"
  Energy and Buildings
  https://doi.org/10.1016/j.enbuild.2021.111046
  [Safe RL with Lyapunov function in a real building control system.
   Practical deployment of safety constraints similar to our target.]

6. CONTROL BARRIER FUNCTIONS & LYAPUNOV SAFETY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Ames, A.D., Coogan, S., Egerstedt, M., Notomista, G., Sreenath, K. & Tabuada, P. (2019)
  "Control Barrier Functions: Theory and Applications"
  ECC 2019
  https://doi.org/10.23919/ECC.2019.8796030
  [Definitive CBF reference. CBF provides forward-invariance guarantee for
   safe sets — formal alternative to shadow mode's heuristic switching.]

Berkenkamp, F., Turchetta, M., Schoellig, A. & Krause, A. (2017)
  "Safe Model-Based Reinforcement Learning with Stability Guarantees"
  NeurIPS 2017, arXiv:1705.08551
  https://arxiv.org/abs/1705.08551
  [Lyapunov-based safe exploration with GP model. Provably safe RL with
   formal stability certificate — key theoretical comparator.]

Cheng, R., Orosz, G., Murray, R.M. & Burdick, J.W. (2019)
  "End-to-End Safe Reinforcement Learning through Barrier Functions for
   Safety-Critical Continuous Control Tasks"
  AAAI 2019, arXiv:1903.08792
  https://arxiv.org/abs/1903.08792
  [CBF as differentiable safety layer in RL — trains the policy to respect
   the CBF constraint via QP projection. Relevant to Extension 1.]

Zhao, W., Queralta, J.P. & Westerlund, T. (2020)
  "Sim-to-Real Transfer in Deep Reinforcement Learning for Robotics"
  IEEE SSCI 2020
  https://doi.org/10.1109/SSCI47803.2020.9308468
  [Sim-to-real gap analysis — analogous concerns arise when deploying shadow
   mode trained on PC-Gym simulated CSTR to real plant.]

7. MODEL-BASED RL & UNCERTAINTY QUANTIFICATION
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Chua, K., Calandra, R., McAllister, R. & Levine, S. (2018)
  "Deep Reinforcement Learning in a Handful of Trials using Probabilistic
   Dynamics Models" (PETS)
  NeurIPS 2018, arXiv:1805.12114
  https://arxiv.org/abs/1805.12114
  [PETS: ensemble of probabilistic NNs for dynamics model, CEM for planning.
   Relevant to Extension 3 — ensemble approach for epistemic uncertainty.]

Nagabandi, A., Kahn, G., Fearing, R.S. & Levine, S. (2018)
  "Neural Network Dynamics for Model-Based Deep Reinforcement Learning with
   Model-Free Fine-Tuning"
  ICRA 2018, arXiv:1708.02596
  https://arxiv.org/abs/1708.02596
  [Hybrid MB+MF RL — train on model, fine-tune on real rollouts.
   Useful for reducing sample complexity in PSE shadow mode training.]

Lakshminarayanan, B., Pritzel, A. & Blundell, C. (2017)
  "Simple and Scalable Predictive Uncertainty Estimation using Deep Ensembles"
  NeurIPS 2017, arXiv:1612.01474
  https://arxiv.org/abs/1612.01474
  [Deep Ensembles: K randomised NNs for uncertainty estimation. Foundational
   reference for Extension 3's ensemble Q-network approach.]

Mohan, A., Mowbray, M., del Rio-Chanona, E.A. & Petsagkourakis, P. (2023)
  "Model-Based Reinforcement Learning for CSTR Control: A Comparative Study"
  Computer Aided Chemical Engineering
  https://doi.org/10.1016/B978-0-443-15274-0.50109-X
  [MBRL applied specifically to CSTR — directly comparable environment to
   our PC-Gym implementation.]

8. OFFLINE RL & CONSERVATIVE METHODS (for pre-deployment safety)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Levine, S., Kumar, A., Tucker, G. & Fu, J. (2020)
  "Offline Reinforcement Learning: Tutorial, Review, and Perspectives on Open
   Problems"
  arXiv:2005.01643
  https://arxiv.org/abs/2005.01643
  [Comprehensive offline RL review. Shadow mode naturally generates offline
   data (from baseline) before the RL agent is deployed — offline pretraining
   is a natural extension.]

Kumar, A., Zhou, A., Tucker, G. & Levine, S. (2020)
  "Conservative Q-Learning for Offline Reinforcement Learning" (CQL)
  NeurIPS 2020, arXiv:2006.04779
  https://arxiv.org/abs/2006.04779
  [CQL: penalises Q-values of out-of-distribution actions. Prevents
   extrapolation errors when training critic on baseline-dominated buffer
   in shadow mode — highly relevant to implementation.]

Kidambi, R., Rajeswaran, A., Netrapalli, P. & Joachims, T. (2020)
  "MOReL: Model-Based Offline Reinforcement Learning"
  NeurIPS 2020, arXiv:2005.05951
  https://arxiv.org/abs/2005.05951
  [Model-based offline RL with pessimistic planning — natural fit for
   initialising shadow mode agent from historical plant data.]

Yu, T., Thomas, G., Yu, L., Ermon, S., Zou, J., Levine, S., Finn, C. & Ma, T. (2020)
  "MOPO: Model-Based Offline Policy Optimization"
  NeurIPS 2020, arXiv:2005.13239
  https://arxiv.org/abs/2005.13239
  [MOPO: penalises uncertain model regions (using ensemble disagreement) —
   same uncertainty signal as Extension 3 but for offline pretraining.]

9. ACTOR-CRITIC ALGORITHMS (Technical Background)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Lillicrap, T.P., Hunt, J.J., Pritzel, A., Heess, N., Erez, T., Tassa, Y., Silver, D.
& Wierstra, D. (2016)
  "Continuous Control with Deep Reinforcement Learning" (DDPG)
  ICLR 2016, arXiv:1509.02971
  https://arxiv.org/abs/1509.02971
  [Original DDPG paper — the base algorithm used in shadow_mode.py.]

Fujimoto, S., van Hoof, H. & Meger, D. (2018)
  "Addressing Function Approximation Error in Actor-Critic Methods" (TD3)
  ICML 2018, arXiv:1802.09477
  https://arxiv.org/abs/1802.09477
  [TD3: twin Q-networks + delayed policy updates to fix DDPG overestimation.
   Drop-in upgrade to shadow_mode.py — worth testing as Extension.]

Haarnoja, T., Zhou, A., Abbeel, P. & Levine, S. (2018)
  "Soft Actor-Critic: Off-Policy Maximum Entropy Deep Reinforcement Learning
   with a Stochastic Actor" (SAC)
  ICML 2018, arXiv:1801.01290
  https://arxiv.org/abs/1801.01290
  [SAC: entropy-regularised RL with automatic temperature tuning. State of the
   art off-policy algorithm; alternative to DDPG for shadow mode agent.]

Schulman, J., Wolski, F., Dhariwal, P., Radford, A. & Klimov, O. (2017)
  "Proximal Policy Optimization Algorithms" (PPO)
  arXiv:1707.06347
  https://arxiv.org/abs/1707.06347
  [Original PPO paper — on-policy baseline used in example_pcgym.py.
   Cannot directly provide Q-values so not directly applicable to Q-value
   switching mode, but relevant for agent-decision mode comparison.]
